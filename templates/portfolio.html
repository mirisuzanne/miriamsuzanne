Hello World:

Eric Meyer is a multimedia artist with experience in theatre, music, film, writing, design, photography, & illustration. He specializes in collaborative process, leading creative teams in the development of new work.

Formerly the Artistic Director of New World Arts and Technical Director of The LIDA Project, Eric is now the lead designer at OddBird, a musician with Teacup Gorilla writer/producer/performer with Vicious Trap, creator of the Susy responsive grid framework, and a core developer on the Compass project.

Also on the team: Sondra Eisenstat is a marketing consultant, writer, and designer who specializes in brand development; and Aaron Liechty is a writer, performer, and illustrator.

I also use the freely available Silkscreen by Jason Kottke and Chunk Five by The League of Moveable Type as well as various fonts pulled directly from your device.
